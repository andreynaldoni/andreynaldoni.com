A little bit about me

First of all, thank you for visiting my website, having you here is awesome! Let me introduce myself, I'm Andrey, 22 years old, Software Developer (a near future Engineer insert_emoticon) since the time I got my first computer in 2002... ~ Fastforwarding sound ~ In 2007 everything starded to be interesting, broadband connection services had finally arrived at my neighborhood \o/, with Internet at home I could start to learn how to code...

It began with games (Counter Strike Mods with AMX MOD X), soon was HTML, later a little bit of Adobe's Action Script and so on... ~ Jumping blocks ~ Well, later things finally got serious, I've decided to attend a Technical Course (from ETEC, a public institution very popular here at Sao Paulo state), only to see if I would "fit" as a Programmer, by the time I was just finishing high school and needed to decide what who I would want to be as an adult, haha...

At ETEC I've saw the beauty of being a Software Developer, I've learned/learnt C++, JAVA, VB .NET, PHP, HTML, CSS [...], but the best part of this period was meet these amazing people: Eduardo (Industrial Mecanic), Simone (HR Professional), Kaio (Geek enthusiast), Yunes (another Geek enthusiast) and my future wife Monyza (that's another story), well, they made me a Software Developer, I had the pleasure to build an outstanding project (yes, I'm sacrificing adjectives I know, I know...) in 6 months! A great epsode of my life and I'm always glad to remember.

I'd say that in the middle of this story I engaged in some social projects, some with special people, got a medal, got a first job as a Programmer, I've also started to attend college (FATEC, also public and very known here) and other lovely things such as my cultural exchange to Canada, but these stories my friend, I'll have to ask me to tell you, for now, "You shall continue to scroll this page".

PS.: I tried to make it small.
